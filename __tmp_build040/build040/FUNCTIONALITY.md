# NEXUS ONE Functionality

## Working interactions
- Header navigation scrolls to each section.
- Mobile menu opens, closes, and collapses after navigation.
- Phone links launch the dialer.
- Email links open the user email client.
- Hero carousel auto-rotates and supports previous, next, slide selection, pause, and play.
- Every service rail and service card opens booking with the matching service preselected.
- Patient trip-status menu opens an explanatory workflow dialog.
- Coverage action opens booking.
- Facility portal cards and preview controls open feature details and partnership actions.
- Quality, compliance, and mechanical-failure cards open detailed workflows.
- Booking form validates required fields, creates a reference, provides an email submission link, copies the summary, and resets.
- Facility partnership form validates fields, creates an email submission link, copies the summary, and resets.
- Accessibility control toggles larger text, high contrast, and reduced motion.
- Dialogs close by close button, outside click, or Escape.

## Production integrations still required
- Secure HIPAA-aware form API and database.
- Authenticated patient, facility, driver, dispatch, and executive portals.
- SMS/email provider integration.
- Live GPS and ETA data.
- Billing, CRM, and document-management integrations.
