Accessibility statement

Nexus Medical Transit is committed to providing digital services that are accessible to people with disabilities.

Conformance target

This website is being developed to support the Revised Section 508 Standards and WCAG 2.2 Level AA. Accessibility is an ongoing process that includes automated testing, keyboard testing, screen-reader review, zoom and reflow testing, and usability testing.

Need assistance?

To request information in an alternative format, report an accessibility barrier, or receive help using the website, contact Nexus Medical Transit:

  • Phone: (888) 760-4990
  • Email: contact@nexusmt.com

Feedback

When reporting an issue, please include the page or feature involved, the assistive technology and browser used, and a description of the problem.
