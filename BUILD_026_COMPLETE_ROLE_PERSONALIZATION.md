# Build 026 — Complete role-personalized Livecare

Livecare now customizes both the moving fleet map and the body workspace for every authorized role.

- Patient/caregiver: one verified ride, timeline, vehicle, ETA, messaging and privacy controls.
- Facility: only facility-linked vehicles and rides, patient history, operations KPIs and exceptions.
- Driver: only assigned vehicle/routes, minimum necessary rider details, instructions and assignment history.
- Dispatcher: system-wide fleet, active assignments, patients, drivers, vehicles, exceptions and searchable history.
- Administrator: enterprise-wide operations and links to administration and dispatch.
- Executive: de-identified network performance and service history.
- QA: quality review queue, status, vehicle/driver context and audit-oriented history.
- Billing: billing-oriented trip activity and searchable authorized history.

The homepage hero sizing and typography remain the global page standard.
